# Example: Pocari Sweat

## Input brief

- Brand: Pocari Sweat
- Product: Pocari Sweat hydration drink
- Target Audience: women athletes aged 18-25
- Consumer Problem: exhausted, thirsty, and unfocused after outdoor exercise in hot weather
- Desired Transformation: feel refreshed, rehydrated, and focused again after one drink
- Tone: BRIGHT + SPORTY
- Text Language: Korean
- Board Title / Main Headline: 한 모금의 리셋

## Reasonable assumptions

- Product Category: Beverage
- Usage Occasion: outdoor track training in summer heat
- Key Benefit: refreshing hydration that restores energy and focus
- Story Structure: CONTRAST
- Color System: hot orange before, cool blue after

## Example Key Idea

- English: A world frozen by heat -> one refreshing drink -> reset into motion.
- Korean concept line: 더위로 멈춘 세계 -> 한 모금 -> 리셋

## Example board logic

- TOP: hero visual of the athlete drinking the product at the peak transformation moment, with the headline 한 모금의 리셋.
- MIDDLE: exactly 10 panels showing heat fatigue, discovery, drinking, and energetic recovery.
- BOTTOM: clean Pocari Sweat packshot, brand logo, and tagline.
